SERVICES

We build software using an agile, iterative development method. Instead of diving straight into coding, we start with in-depth design and strategy session that helps us make projects more cost effective, and helps us build the right thing, every time. Once that’s done, the coding magic begins!
We provide data security for our clients.Data security is important for all offices. One threat to watch out for is ransomware. Professional hackers target offices hoping to hold your data ransom in exchange for cash. The public messaging that follows a ransomware hit usually includes phrases like “sophisticated” and “targeted attack.”
We provide E-Mail services for the clients. The foundation of all work communication for many of us, email is of utmost importance. We must keep accounts secure, store email data as needed, make sure the software is working smoothly, add or restrict email access. Our preferred email service for clients is Microsoft 365.
File servers are computers large enough to hold all the data generated by your office – that could be emails,or any other kind of work you do. Traditionally, offices have purchased file servers and kept them in your physical office. The Cloud Office system we provide our clients (and use ourselves) has many benefits in addition to cost savings, and it allows you to log in to your entire work computer on any device.
